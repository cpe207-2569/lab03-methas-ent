Mae Kae Waterfall

A small, beautiful limestone waterfall hidden within Tham Pha Thai National Park. It features stunning, crystal-clear emerald water cascading down gentle tiers amidst a lush forest. The atmosphere is shaded and peaceful, making it perfect for relaxing, picnicking, and swimming.

Wat Tha Ton

A magnificent hilltop temple spanning across nine peaks in Mae Ai district. Known for its stunning Crystal Pagoda (Chedi Kaew) and giant Buddha statues, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Mae Kok River, the valley, and the surrounding misty mountains.

View Details
Doi Lang

A serene mountain peak along the Thai-Myanmar border, renowned as a world-class paradise for bird watchers and nature photographers. It offers cool weather year-round, spectacular morning seas of mist, and pristine wilderness away from the crowds.

View Details
Phi Phi Islands

A world-famous tropical archipelago in the Andaman Sea, renowned for its towering limestone cliffs, pristine white-sand beaches, and emerald waters. Home to the iconic Maya Bay, it is the ultimate destination for island hopping, snorkeling, and diving.
